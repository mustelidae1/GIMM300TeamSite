About Our Team

We are the Interdimensionals, a team of skilled and deadly internet commandos with flexibility within the space-time.

The origins of our group are shrouded in myth, but some say we were created amongst a cramped classroom full of stressed students.

Others claim we are the last beings from a different reality entirely, sent to this universe to make aesthetically pleasing websites.

Whatever the myths may be, there are two things known for certain: the Interdimensionals wield a blade of awesome power named “Bootstrap”, and they take no prisoners.
